Flightless Lovebirds

Flightless Lovebirds was my Freshers 2023 GameJam submission, and my first experience with Unreal Engine. Due to this project being developed over only a day of real time, and the fact that I was still moving in and growing accustomed to life at University, I was only able to dedicate a couple of hours to it. It was made with a team, with me handling the sound effects, background, coding, player sprite and level design.
